Little information about Malta

I like Malta, because here I feel energy of see.
I imagine I'm going back to old times or movies when I see old yellow buildings,
beautiful balcons and doors.

Malta is not one island – it is an archipelago.

The Maltese language is a mix of Arabic, French & English.

There are no forests, mountains or rivers in Malta.

It is a popular filming location for big-budget productions such as Gladiator, Captain Philips, Assassins Creed, Troy, The Da Vinci Code and Season 1 of Games of Thrones.
